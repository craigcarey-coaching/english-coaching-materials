How to use this

Timestamps are approximate (taken from the published chapter markers — nudge a few seconds either way on your platform). Each segment runs the same shape: pre-teach the vocabulary, play once for the B1 gist question, play again for the B2 detail questions, then open the discussion. The built-in timer is for the listen itself.

Levels: every segment works at B2; the green B1 items and sentence stems are the on-ramp. Toggle the stems off for stronger groups. Answer keys are teacher-only — they're collapsed and flagged; don't reveal them on a shared screen.

Production wrap-up · reusable

Say it back

B1 path

Choose one segment. In four or five sentences, say what the founders think about it — and whether you agree. Use one new word from the segment's vocabulary.

B2 stretch

Pick the claim you found most arguable and take the opposite side for two minutes. Then write an 80-word position paragraph: state your view, give one reason, concede one point to the other side.
