            Questions they will actually ask

            Record a voice memo. One three-beat line for each. No script.

            1. When can we have the mastered version?
            2. Is the artwork approved by the brand yet?
            3. The perfume campaign goes live on the 20th — can you move the release forward?
            4. Who is clearing the sample with the original artist's estate?
            5. What are we pitching to Spotify editorial, exactly?
            6. Do you have social assets ready, or are we building them?
            7. What happens if the brand rejects the artwork next week?
