🇳🇱 NETHERLANDS & 🇩🇰 DENMARK

Fish Hotels Under the Sea

Tall wind turbines stand in the sea. Above the water, they catch the wind and make energy. 💨

Below the water, something amazing happens: fish, crabs and mussels make homes around the turbines! 🐟🦀

Scientists say new sea animals appear there every year. The turbines are like quiet hotels for fish.

🧠 Where do the fish make their homes?
🇫🇷 FRANCE & 🇬🇧 UK

Flowers for the Bees

Bees are in danger in many countries because they cannot find enough flowers. 🐝

Some solar farms have a clever plan: they plant wildflowers all around the panels. Now the farms are full of happy bees and butterflies! 🌸🦋

The land makes clean energy above — and a safe home for insects below.

🧠 What do the solar farms plant for the bees?
🇳🇴 NORWAY

The Salmon Stairs

Norway makes most of its energy with water power from rivers. But big dams are a problem for salmon — the fish cannot swim past them! 🐟

So engineers build fish ladders: little steps of water next to the dam. The salmon jump up the steps, one by one, and travel home to lay their eggs.

The noisy water splashes, the salmon jump — and then they disappear up the river. Smart fish, smart engineers! 🪜
